Voice AI — Guest Call Scenarios
The AI handles every inbound call. Guests never know it's not a person.
Guest calls: "I can't get in"
AI reads them their door code, confirms the address, offers to resend check-in instructions via text. Escalates to us only if code fails.
Guest calls: "The heat isn't working"
AI logs the issue, creates a maintenance ticket, notifies the HVAC vendor by SMS, and tells the guest someone will be there within 2 hours.
Guest calls: "Can I check out late?"
AI checks the calendar for the next booking. If there's a gap, approves the late checkout automatically. If not, politely declines with the standard time.
Guest calls: "There's a noise complaint"
AI logs the complaint, sends an alert to us, and sends the guest a message reminding them of quiet hours. Flags if Minut noise sensor confirms it.
