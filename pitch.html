We identify high-performing vacation destinations using AirDNA — occupancy rates, average daily rates, and revenue per market. We only target areas where the data confirms strong demand from travelers.

Step 2 — Comp Analysis (The 3 Rules)
We filter listings using a proven 3-rule system that removes noise and finds only real, active, established comps: 250+ days available, 20+ reviews, and active recent review activity. This gives us the true market benchmark.

Voice AI — Example Guest Calls
The AI handles every inbound call. Guests get instant help around the clock.
Guest: "I can't get in"
AI reads the door code, confirms the address, resends check-in instructions by text. Escalates to us only if the code fails.
Guest: "The AC isn't working"
AI logs the issue, creates a maintenance ticket, notifies the HVAC vendor by SMS, and tells the guest someone will be there within 2 hours.
Guest: "Can I check out late?"
AI checks the calendar. If there's a gap before the next booking it approves automatically. If not, it politely declines with the standard checkout time.
Guest: "There's a noise complaint"
AI logs it, alerts us, and sends the guest a message reminding them of quiet hours. Cross-references with noise sensor data from Minut.
